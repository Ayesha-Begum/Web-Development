About Me

I am Ghousia Begum. Born in early 90's. A Pharmacy graduate. From The City of Destiny - Visakhapatnam. Living in Sharjah with my life parter. I love to write any thing to every thing. I am a book worm too.

It all started:

When I was in class 8 inspired by my sister who gave me motivation to write by doing all the crazy stuff and irritating me always.

At the age of 23 my first e-book 'I thought you were mine' was published It gave me lot of strength and positiveness in proceeding further. Day by day I improvised and started writing in the local language Telugu. Many short stories like Ram-Sita, Pooja, Bandhutvam were sent for publications but were never published. I felt bad. But never gave up. The reason behind me never giving up is my supporting family.

I write shahiries too. Participated in a contest named Raindrops on paper conducted by writersmelon blog. And my article with title Father's princess got selected and was published in TOI.
